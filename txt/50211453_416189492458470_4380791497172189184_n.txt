relative clauses (part 5) i. when is used to refer to time, usually after nouns like time, period, moment, year, day, summer etc. it can be replaced by that or can be omitted. e.g. i haven't seen them since the year when they got married. i haven't seen them since the year ( that ) they got married. i . why is used to give reason, usually after the word reason. it can either be replaced by that or can be omitted. .9. the reason why i can't meet you tonight is that i'm working. the reason ( that ) i can't meet you tonight is that i'm working.
